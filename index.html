SLEEP-N-SLEEP Loyalty Program

SLEEP-N-SLEEP Loyalty Program is a tiered rewards initiative designed for hospitality users, including business travelers, leisure guests, and corporate clients. The program offers a points-based system, tiered benefits like Silver Tier perks, and personalized dashboards to enhance user engagement and long-term value.

As Product Manager, I led the end-to-end design and launch of the program. I validated the concept through user research and competitive analysis, then translated insights into a detailed PRD and clear MVP scope. I structured a roadmap covering research, development, and post-launch phases, collaborating with cross-functional teams to deliver backend infrastructure and user-facing features. The go-to-market strategy aligned with business goals, using segmented messaging and value-driven rewards to drive adoption, engagement, and measurable growth.

POSPadi

POSPadi is a fintech solution designed to provide low-cost, intuitive POS services for Nigerian merchants, facilitating everyday financial transactions like bill payments and transfers. I worked alongside the product team to shape a vision grounded in accessibility and inclusion.

Through targeted user research and competitive analysis, we validated market need and crafted a roadmap that addressed both technical and user-centric priorities. I focused on defining clear user stories and PRDs, aligning them with achievable sprint goals. Offline support and multilingual capabilities were prioritized based on merchant feedback. Regular syncs with cross-functional teams ensured alignment across design, engineering, and QA. Every phase—from ideation to delivery—was guided by outcome-oriented milestones, ensuring product objectives remained realistic, actionable, and tightly scoped.

This is my original concept, and I’m currently building the website as the first step of the development process. SmartSave is an ongoing project, and I’m refining it based on user needs to ensure it’s simple, helpful, and easy to use.
